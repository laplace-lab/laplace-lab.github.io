ISERP information

  • Most of us are employed by the Institute for Social and Economic Research and Policy (ISERP).
  • The administrative offices (Suite 370) are difficult to find, watch video on the page: ISERP directions. It has a groovy sound track.
  • Useful email aliases include:
    • Hiring, HR, salary etc...: iserp-hiring@columbia.edu
    • Grant funding both in process, awarded and reimbursements: iserp-funding@columbia.edu
    • General questions: iserp@columbia.edu
  • Direct deposit--highly reccomended otherwise you can pick up checks at ISERP which is currently closed.
  • Reimbursement for travel or other work expenses involves a TBER form. Do not let expenses get too old (120 days?) or they will tax the rembursement.
